Website & Business Goals

4 major uncovered site opportunities and business goals my 2024 optimisation strategy

  1. TriTerm, UHOne's most profitable product was being sunset by end of year
  2. There was major user confusion about products shown on product list pages due to variable product availability and lack of product intent capture
  3. Initial form entry conversion rate was unacceptably low
  4. Return vsitors not only converted quickly and high on pages but also progressed very effectively through the funnel after initial engagement

Year In Review

In 2024, site analytics, survey data, industry trends, and competitive auditing produced 4 key strategic pillars for optimizing the UHOne site:

  1. TriTerm promotion: Selling TriTerm, UHOne's highest margin product, prior to its sunset
  2. Conecting UHOne: Ensuring a seamless experience from beginning to end of funnel
  3. Design & UX: General design optimization
  4. New vs Return Personalization: Tailoring content and functionality to user intent and previous interactions

To action on these strategic pillars, I planned 21 initiatives and executed 39 tests. I also stayed nimble with 7 ad-hoc initiatives.

By committing to strategic pillars, I was able to make measurable progress on key customer pain points and business goals

Our comprehensive approach led to $8.7M incremental revenue, but that's not all

  • Visits impacted: 1,900,000
  • Conversions in tests: 226,000
  • Test win rate: 51%
